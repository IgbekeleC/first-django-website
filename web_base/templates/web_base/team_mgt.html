Our History

MICB-Tech Ltd was incorporated in Oct. 2017 as a tech company with focus in agriculture, food processing and other agro businesses. In order to fully exploit its tech-potential and meet the increased demand on software solutions among small and medium enterprises, the scope of the business operations was expanded early 2019 to include Software development, marketing and marketing research, and much more.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to be one of the leading software and tech-based marketing solutions for businesses, across the country while leveraging on innovative ideas and people.

Vision Statement

MICB-Tech is passionately committed to making modern and effective customized applications for businesses at an affordable price.

Our Culture and Values

MICB Tech's culture makes it possible for it to be outstanding in its deliverables and it is the key to its success. Our values are subsets of our culture.

These core values are:

- Excellence
- Professionalism
- Integrity
- Customer-centric.
